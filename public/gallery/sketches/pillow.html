Pillow

Originally drawn: 4 April 2025

One day, a squirrel woke me up, I got this idea, and then I went back to bed. The idea stuck in my head for two days until I doodled it during yet another one of my many group meetings for an assignment. (It lasted four hours!)

Tools used: blue Snowman 0.1 drawing pen and Tombow Fudenosuke for lines, Tombow dual brush pens for colours.
