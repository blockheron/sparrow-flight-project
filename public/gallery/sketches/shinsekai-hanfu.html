Shinsekai Hanfu

Originally drawn: 19 March 2025

I've been in a play-by-post text-based session thing with one of Helvetia's other players for a really long time now. The session's reaching 3000 messages! Anyway, the birdfam are currently in Japan because the next session is going to be a masquerade hosted there, and Gavrill decided to take his kids and a close friend, Elmira :) for a week-long trip.

The group just stopped by a market, where my friend picked out hanfu for Hrodwyn and Gavrill to wear. I decided to sketch it real quick as a break from my computer graphics assignment, during a group meeting where one person came late. (You bet Gavrill's going to try file this under company expenses.) (And yes, Gavrill's hair is longer! He finally had a barber neaten it up, too)

Tools: Snowman red, blue, and black 0.1 darwing pens, Tombow dual brush pens, correction fluid. Minor fixes made using Clip Studio Paint.
