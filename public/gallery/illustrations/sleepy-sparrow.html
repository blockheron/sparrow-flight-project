Sleepy Sparrow

Originally drawn: 3 May 2025

I wanted to make a drawing for a hiatus announcement, and I've always wanted to make a drawing of the birdfam together in my comic style for the site. So, I merged both ideas! (And it only took 1.5 years to draw the family together in colour!!)

The original idea's concept was the same as the final's, except it was meant to be landscape and show the Vorobyevs in their kitchen. I also decided to add Steora to balance out the composition, and because she visits so often, she practically ends up being part of the family :)
